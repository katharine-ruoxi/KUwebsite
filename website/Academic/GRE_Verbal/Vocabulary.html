Vocabulary Questions

1. Which one is not the meaning of "abandon"
(A) carefree, freedom from constraint
(B) to put an end to (something planned or previously agreed to)
(C) to give (oneself) over unrestrainedly
(D) to give up sth
(E) to to withdraw from often in the face of danger or encroachment

2. Meaning of "abash"
(A) to lower in rank, office, prestige, or esteem
(B) to destroy the self-possession or self-confidence of ,disconcert, embarrass
(C) to reduce in degree or intensity
(D) to reduce in amount or value
(E) to put an end to

3.Which word means "to make briefer"
(A) abase
(B) abdicate
(C) abbreviate
(D) abate
(E) abash

4.Which one(s) is/are the antonym(s) of "aberrant"
(A) peculiar
(B) anomalous
(C) typical
(D) unwonted
(E) singular

5. Which one(s) is/are the meaning(s) of "abet"
(A) to assist or support in the achievement of a purpose
(B) temporary inactivity
(C) to renounce a throne,to relinquish (power or responsibility) formally
(D) to actively encourage (as an activity or plan)
(E) to regard with extreme repugnance

Answers: D,A,C,C,AD
