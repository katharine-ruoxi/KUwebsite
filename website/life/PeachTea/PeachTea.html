Peach Tea

Ingredients：
  • Peach (1)
  • Black tea bag (1)
  • Sugar (1 Tbsp)
  • Water (2 Cups)
Tools:
  • Spoon
  • Cup
  • Squeezer
Instructions:
    1.Brew the black tea and pour it in the cup.
    2.Wash the peach to make sure it is clean.
    3.Cut the peach into two halves and remove the core.
    4. Use one of the halves and cut a few slices for decoration.
    5. Squeeze the rest peach and add into the brewed tea.
    6. Mix 1 Tbsp sugar in the tea, you can add more/less according to your taste.
    7. Use the decoration peach pieces to decorate.
    8. Enjoy it! Or you can use fridge to cool it down and drink it~
