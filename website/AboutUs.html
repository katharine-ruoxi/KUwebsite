Our History

Started in 2009, Katharine University quickly established itself as a hub to help people who want to be successful. We especially focused on college students, and grad students. Now as we growing, we also get involved in high school, middle school education, as well as junior professionals. Unlike most of the services in the market, this is a VIP only service with affordable price.
